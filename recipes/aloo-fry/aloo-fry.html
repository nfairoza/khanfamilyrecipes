Aloo Fry

from the kitchen of Noor

A quick weekday side · crisp potatoes, ready fast

What you'll need

  • potatoes, peeled & cut into small pieces
  • olive oil, for frying
  • mustard seeds (rai)
  • cumin seeds (zira)
  • green chilies, for flavour
  • turmeric (haldi), a good pinch
  • red chili powder, to finish
  • salt (namak) — added at the end

Salt at the END for crisp potatoes — salting early draws out water and softens them. Amounts by feel.

How we make it

  1. Temper the oil. Heat olive oil and add the mustard seeds and cumin. Wait until they splutter.
  2. Green chilies. Add the green chilies for flavour.
  3. Add the potatoes. Tip in the cut potatoes and the turmeric. Toss to coat.
  4. Fry crisp. Fry on medium-high heat, stirring often, until the potatoes are cooked all the way through and as crisp as you like.
  5. Season to finish. Only now add the red chili powder and salt. Toss through.
  6. Serve. Lovely with warm rice, a spoon of ghee and dal.
Amma's note: don't rush the frying and don't crowd the pan — that's how you get crisp edges instead of steamed potatoes. Keep the salt for the very end.
