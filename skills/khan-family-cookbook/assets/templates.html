Chicken Dum Biryani

from the kitchen of Noor

Feeds a full table · Sunday project

Every Eid, the whole house smelled of this by ten in the morning — Amma would seal the pot with dough and dare anyone to open it early. This is her biryani the way she actually made it, not the way she said she made it.

Ingredients

  • basmati rice, a generous pot's worth
  • chicken, on the bone
  • fried onions — more than feels wise
  • chili to your family's bravery

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken until it forgives you for the wait.
  2. Parboil the rice — a grain should bend, not break.
  3. Layer, seal, and put it on dum. You will know when it's ready: the kitchen smells unbearable in the best way.
Amma's note: add extra ghee for whoever you love most.

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken until it forgives you for the wait.
  2. Parboil the rice — a grain should bend, not break.
  3. Fry the onions darker than feels safe.
  4. Layer: rice, chicken, onions, mint — twice.
  5. Seal the lid with dough. No peeking.
  6. Dum on the lowest flame while the house fills with it.
  7. Rest ten minutes with the seal unbroken.
  8. Open at the table, always at the table.
Amma's note: add extra ghee for whoever you love most.
